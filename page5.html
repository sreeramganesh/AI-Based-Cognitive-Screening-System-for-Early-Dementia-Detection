AI Tool for Early-Stage Dementia Detection

Welcome to the AI-Based Cognitive Screening System. This tool helps assess early signs of cognitive changes through speech, behavior, and reasoning tasks.

Before You Begin:

  • Find a quiet place with minimal background noise.
  • Ensure your microphone and camera are working.
  • Answer each task calmly — there are no right or wrong answers.
  • The session takes around 15–20 minutes.
  • Your responses are confidential and used only for analysis.
